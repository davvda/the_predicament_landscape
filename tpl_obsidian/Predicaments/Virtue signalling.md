**Virtue signalling is acting disingenuously in ways that give the appearance of good, righteous, or moral character**. In a society of spectacle and appearance it seems to be increasingly important to simply appear virtuous, most often in the absence of any virtuous acts. I wonder what filter we'll put on our Facebook profile pictures when the oceans start boiling.
