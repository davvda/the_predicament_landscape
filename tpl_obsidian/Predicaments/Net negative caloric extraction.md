In the U.S. [approximately 7 calories are used to produce 1 calorie of food](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/303895662_The_Food_and_Farming_Transition) energy. This ratio combined with the [exosomatic energy use](http://www.ejolt.org/2012/12/human-energy-use-endosomatic-exosomatic/) presents a picture of a highly **in**efficient food system. For a civilisation obsessed with inefficiency we sure suck at it.
