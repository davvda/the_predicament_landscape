Connectivity, as a predicament, **refers to the modern world's incessant binding together of all humans through the internet**, its incentives to never disconnect and, consequently, the resulting tendency to be disconnected from everything else. When you seemingly have the entire world at your fingertips, why settle for staring out of the train window? The computer, in many ways, introduced the end of real experience, provided an alternative to life, facilitated the ultimate unconviviality.
