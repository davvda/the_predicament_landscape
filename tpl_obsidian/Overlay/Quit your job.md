> I signed a contract for a job yesterday. It's a good job. In fact, it's a better job than I have any right to expect being able to get. Many stories and myths emphasise the return. The hero left for some great adventure and then returns, a different person, hopefully. I left my previous employment because the dissonance between what I sensed in the world and the bullshit of the work crushed me.
