The Exchange from Always Coming Home?

> **Salvage** the good things we have a chance of taking with us; **mourn** the good things we're not able to take with us, and tell their stories; **notice** the things that were never as good as we told each other they were, and the chance we're being given to leave these behind; and **pick up the dropped threads** from earlier in the story, the practices that were marked as "old-fashioned", "inefficient" or "obsolete", but which may yet make all the difference.
