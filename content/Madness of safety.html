Madness of safety

Safety means being protected from harm or danger. In our risk-assessment of the world we must've taken a wrong turn somewhere. Streetlights everywhere, security cameras, padded playgrounds, kids driven to school, and decreasing radii of kids' play areas. Meanwhile decreasing air quality and nutrition density, unceasing global warming, and unquestioned development of cities prioritising cars. What the actual fuck are we doing?
