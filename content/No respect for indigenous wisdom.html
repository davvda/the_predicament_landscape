No respect for indigenous wisdom

Many indigenous peoples have (or had) existed for a nearly unimaginably long time, often in harmony with their surroundings and living fairly peaceful lives, especially when considering peace and violence in a broader sense. There are, of course, exceptions. Most indigenous wisdom will appear quite foreign and nonsensical to most westerners, both less and more abstract, less and more real. The strangeness of their wisdom and the exceptions where violence and brutishness was the norm has nearly led to an extinction of indigenous wisdom traditions and a complete lack of respect for it.
