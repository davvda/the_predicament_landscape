Globalisation

Globalisation is the worldwide process of interaction and integration among people, organisations, cultures, services, and more. Globalisation is sometimes seen as the stage following the (French) term mondialisation where it implies "the dissolution of national identities and the abolishment of borders inside the world network of economic exchanges". Colonialism has softened its edges and strengthened its pull, an all-encompassing vortex.
