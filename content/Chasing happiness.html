Chasing happiness

Much misery comes from chasing happiness. Yet, there are ways of fulfilment we, the people born of modernity, can't even begin to imagine. Are you happy? Does it matter?

Also, happiness is the one great promise of modernity, as long as you chase it within its boundaries, through its own means of chasing.
