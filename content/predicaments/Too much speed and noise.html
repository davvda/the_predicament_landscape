Too much speed and noise

Everything moves fast cracked highway asphalt flowing bubbling venomous. Mouth moves. Silent cacophony of voices from exhaustion pipes. Loud static highest gear. Can you hear the light?

Things appear to be moving with such speed and it's increasingly difficult to make sense of it through all the noise. Much of the noise comes from us moving rapidly, going nowhere. We miss the changes of the seasons. I wish we would get to nowhere. Nowhere is slow, nowhere is quiet.
