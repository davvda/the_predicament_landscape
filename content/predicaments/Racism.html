Racism is the discrimination of people based on their "race", appearance, ethnicity, or other, commonly visual and place-related, characteristics. It is also one of the more prevalent shortcuts to passing blame and striving for betterment. If they simply went away we'd be better off. Racism easily takes hold of ancient instincts to oppose strangers and be weary of people who appear different. This makes racism a suitable driving force for many political agendas, propaganda, and directing the rage of a people towards a common (non-)enemy.
