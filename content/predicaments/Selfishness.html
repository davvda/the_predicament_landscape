Selfishness

Selfishness is the exclusive regard to one's own interest and happiness. Selfishness is not particularly discouraged in modernity, quite the opposite, sometimes it is even regarded as the virtuous root of human nature and consequently the cause of social progress. In a way, capitalism capitalises on humans selfishness in order to create more wealth for others to succeed in being selfish. Tens of thousands of years of wisdom pointing to selfishness as one of, if not the main struggle of human life, and yet here we are wondering why things aren't going the way we hoped...
