Madness of safety

Safety means being protected from harm or danger. In our risk-assessment of the world we must've taken a wrong turn somewhere. Streetlights everywhere, security cameras, padded playgrounds, kids driven to school, and decreasing radii of kids' play areas. Meanwhile, worsening air quality and nutrition density, unceasing climate change, dysfunctional educations shaping children's minds into machine components, and unquestioned development of urban environments prioritising cars over people. What the actual fuck are we doing?
