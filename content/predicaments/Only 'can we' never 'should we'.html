Only 'can we' never 'should we'

The road to hell is paved with good intentions.

Humans' natural instinct and curiosity to ask "can we?" is, has been, and will continue to be one of our biggest assets. However, the world that "can we?" conjures must now more than ever be balanced by the wisdom of "should we?". The risk we face with many current developments is that it only takes one "can we?" for it to be the last.
