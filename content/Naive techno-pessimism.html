Naive techno-pessimism

Techno-pessimists tend to believe that none of the problems of modernity can be solved by new technology and, often, that technology in general is the main problem. Technology in a broader sense is and will always be vital for humanity to have a place on Earth. We do, however, need to become wiser in our development of technologies, their tools, and their use. Technology is, after all, not values neutral.

It doesn't stop being magic just because you know how it works.


Terry Pratchett
