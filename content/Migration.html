Migration

Migration is the movement of people from one place to another, with the purpose of permanently or temporarily settling at the new location. It is seldom voluntary in as much as it is forced due to worsening conditions in the place people are leaving.

Outgoing connections:

  • Apathy - Losing one's connection to a place can uproot all sense of care.
  • Avoiding uncertainty - Not unreasonably, simply leaving places with uncertain or no future.
  • Blaming the people - The west sends bombs to countries and then get angry when the people fleeing the bombs arrive at their doorsteps.
  • Carelessness
  • Culture wars
  • Genocide - Sometimes, genocide can take the form of slowly forced migration. Humans might survive but the people has died.
  • Globalisation
  • Government - Most modern governments have been dumbfounded by the issues related to large-scale migration.
  • Hemispherical asymmetry (Earth)
  • Hobbesian views - The life of a refugee is not an easy one.
  • Identity
  • Ineffective institutions
  • Inequality
  • Lack of agency - Takes one key decision to leave, takes a generation or more to build up a new life.
  • Lack of belonging
  • Predicaments/Language|Language
  • Mistreatment of other humans
  • Modern slavery
  • National borders - Increased migration tends to strengthen borders.
  • Polarisation
  • Racism - Just speculations here... If the only people you encounter from a certain ethnicity have a background of several years spent fleeing wars, bringing with them all that that entails, these people will most likely have wounds that radiate outwards, hardening your prejudices towards them.
  • Social erosion
  • Surveillance - Keep a close look at the newcomers.
  • Unemployment
  • Views on human nature
  • Violence
  • Waste - Not infrequently, the first generation arriving in a new place is wasted away despite its failed attempts to adapt. The first children born without the memories of war and destruction of home might be able to find their place, even so, the ancestral trauma continues to trickle down and manifest itself in all sorts of problematic tendencies.
