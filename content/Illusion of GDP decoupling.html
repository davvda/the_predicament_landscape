Illusion of GDP decoupling

GDP decoupling tends to mean the decoupling of GDP (or growth) from carbon emissions. Taking a broader view would mean decoupling GDP from any long-term detrimental externalities. Taking an even broader view could mean increasing global welfare without increasing long-term detrimental externalities and, despite the predicament's title, this is not an unreasonable goal for humanity to have for the coming years, ideally maintaining or increasing welfare while decreasing externalities. Now that is an impressive optimisation challenge if I've ever seen one.

It's easier to imagine the end of the world than the end of capitalism.


Mark Fisher
