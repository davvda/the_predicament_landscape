Lack of humility

Humility means an understanding of our place in the web of life, a willingness to see truth, and an openness to the value and meaning of the seemingly meaningless. Being humble is being firmly planted in the ground of reality.

We humans have undoubtedly, in some ways, gotten far along certain paths of progress or thought-manifestations. However, we lose our humility when our feet leave the humus of the Earth. A tree that reaches towards the heavens while forsaking its roots quickly dies. There is no honour in mere grandeur, we must keep our feet and roots in the ground of reality.

Outgoing connections:

  • Carelessness
  • Unheeded warnings
  • Ignorance of ongoing collapse
  • Lack of historical insight
  • Delusional domination of nature
  • Avoiding uncertainty
  • Illusion of competence
  • Illusion of understanding
  • Animal views
