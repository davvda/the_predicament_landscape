Ignoring diversity

Our ability to reach unity in diversity will be the beauty and the test of our civilisation.


Mahatma Gandhi

People, places, cultures, thoughts, opinions, beliefs, systems, values, rights, morals, ethics, are diverse, they're different. There will never be one way of life for all to follow, neither should there be. Equality must include diversity to be of value. The only functioning way for all of humanity to be the same is having the freedom to be different, to be diverse. The next big thing will be many small things.

I think... if it is true that there are as many minds as there are heads, then there are as many kinds of love as there are hearts.


Leo Tolstoy, Anna Karenina
