Polarisation

Once they have you asking the wrong questions they don't have to worry about the answers.


Thomas Pynchon, Gravity's Rainbow

Polarisation means divergence from the centre towards the end of an axis. In politics this tends to mean that groups move towards opposing ideological extremes, right and left extremism, but polarisation can mean any separation, segregation, or divergence of groups from a joint centre to opposing extremes.
