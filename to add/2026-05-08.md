# Entering the Borderlands
![[apl_monogrey 1.svg]]
We've officially entered the borderlands. Expressed in a different way, I consider my Predicament Landscape sufficiently *finished*! In [[In The Borderlands]] we dwell shortly in the space between the **predicaments** and the **overlay**. It's been fun to look into the order created from several years of chaotic writing and deep reflecting. As intended and hoped, an order erupted from the chaos, clear enough to provide me with some insights about our predicament landscape, despite the limitation of really only having one main cartographer, me.

Should I somehow squeeze the previous update article (Insights from the Landscape) into this space? I am unsure of how it would fit but there are definitely conclusions, vague or less so, in those concepts that aren't represented by the current [[In The Borderlands]] section. I'll see what I can do.

Looking back at the many early mornings of thinking, researching, brainstorming, writing, connecting, and espresso-drinking, where did we end up? Well, **250** interconnected predicaments with a total of nearly **7700** links, many of which have link motivations written for them. Do I find it holistic enough? Well, as holistic as an explicit representation of these reflections can be, sure. Am I happy with it? Yes, I think so. Though, it feels a bit like it has been more of an obsession or a compulsion rather than a labour of love. It had to be done. I had to do it. If nothing else in order to ensure my own sanity or verify a lack thereof. The Overlay, however, does feel like a labour of love. This is also why it'll most likely be written at an even slower pace, letting each blade of thought acquire proper flower-status.

If you know someone who might find some answers or arrive at better questions by exploring this landscape, feel free to share either the starting page or [[In The Borderlands]]. Also, feel free to get in touch. Exploring something so vast can get lonely. 

*Blessings to you all,
from where the forest begins*
