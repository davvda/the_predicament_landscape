Identity

Identity is the set of qualities, beliefs, personality traits, appearance, and/or expressions that characterise a person or a group. Identity politics has become one of the western world's biggest distraction. Interestingly, a focus on identity can bring one closer together to people and makes one's societal interconnectedness obvious. Identity in this case moves people away from individualism and towards something akin to collectivism. What makes this more of a predicament than a benefit is that while a focus on identity brings one closer to those whose identities are similar (or identical) to ones own, it moves one further away from everyone else.
