Genocide

Genocide is the intentional destruction of a people. People, cultures, and traditions whose voices must be silenced for the greater good of modernity have two options. Assimilate and forget or be annihilated. There are very few peoples left to annihilate but many, perhaps most, contemporary military conflicts have genocidal traits.
