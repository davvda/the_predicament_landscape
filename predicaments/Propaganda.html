Propaganda

Propaganda is a way of propagating an agenda through persuading and influencing an audience's emotions, attitudes, opinions, and actions. Propaganda takes many forms, some obvious and explicit such as social media manipulation and political campaigns, others hidden and deeply ingrained in education, cultures, news, narratives, science, and religion. It is an act of non-kinetic violence.

Assumptions or opinions are like computer programs in people’s minds. Those programs take over against the best of intentions — they produce their own intentions.

David Bohm
