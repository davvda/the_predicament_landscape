Access to food

Access to food means having access to the necessary energy and nutrients to live. This means living in a place where one can grow fruits and vegetables, one can hunt or fish, raise livestock, forage and gather wild plants and fungi, or have food transported to one's location.

When I give food to the poor, they call me a saint. When I ask why the poor have no food, they call me a communist.

Dom Helder Camara
