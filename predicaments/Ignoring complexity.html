Ignoring complexity

Ignore complexity and with time nothing will make sense to you, nothing will be coherent with your thoughts, consistent with your actions, in line with your beliefs. You will be a simplistic contradiction in a complex world working against you. Complexity is the concept that bridges our mental abstraction with the infinity of concrete reality. If your abstractions take no account of this bridging with reality you will hit that concrete wall at the speed of stupidity.
