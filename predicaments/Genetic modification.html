Genetic modification

Genetic modification means modifying the underlying genetic blueprint for any organism using technology, usually with the intent of adding desirable traits and/or removing undesirable ones, but also creating novel organisms. It is potentially the final stepping stone in our manipulation-focused mechanistic view of life.
