Lack of cultural control systems

Wisdom, spirituality, animism, and elders have contributed historically to creating cultural control systems which, as opposed to laws, control behaviours with an open hand. Modernity mostly lacks cultural control systems since they have been replaced by game theory. When people mention the need for religion, "a new christianity", or a complete destruction of capitalism, I believe the foundational discussion that must be had is one of cultural control systems in general. A faulty control system will lead to complete lack of cultural balance, an absence of any control system whatsoever is unbounded chaos.
