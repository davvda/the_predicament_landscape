Missing elders

The elders that exist today speak to deaf ears and modernity is not creating more of them. Elders are what keeps cultures together, the binders of links in the chain of time, the silent and sonorous transmitters of wisdom, the generators of future elders. Without elders we are both blind and deaf with nothing to say.
