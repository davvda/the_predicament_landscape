Evil

It is a man's own mind, not his enemy or foe, that lures him to evil ways.

Gautama Buddha

Evil is the exponential externalisation of internal suffering. It is an unleashing of inner demons into the world, their power multiplied by hatred, revenge, destruction, death, pain, apathy, and war. Every person must overcome their own evils.
