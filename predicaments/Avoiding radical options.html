Avoiding radical options

Radical options are generally ways of managing our predicaments that are deemed too unrealistic and ambitious. We shouldn't fear radical options. We must rip out and restructure the very roots of our thoughts, ideas, practices, and ways of life. What must be done to ensure our continued survival is nothing short of radical so if someone claims you're being too radical you might be approaching a path forward.

To be radical is to grasp things by the root.

Karl Marx
