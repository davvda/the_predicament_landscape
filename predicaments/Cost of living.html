Cost of living

The cost of living is the cost of maintaining a certain standard of living. The constant need to pay the cost of living keeps many people away from life. The bottom half, the poorest, of the global population owns 2% of global wealth, while the top 1% owns 38%. When the cost of living is high compared to one's income it then becomes impossible to acquire wealth and, together with the debt from loans, one ends up in a situation that completely lacks agency and optionality, where there is no way out but to keep earning, keep owing, keep paying.
