Energy

Energy is activity and vigour, energy is work, heat, light, flow, electricity, sound. Energy is happiness, pleasure, and living force. Energy is life and all life needs energy. How we get and use energy is the central predicament of existence. Less philosophically, we get our energy from the sun in different forms which we then use and consume in order to live.
