Loneliness

A great fire burns within me, but no one stops to warm themselves at it, and passers-by only see a wisp of smoke.

Vincent Van Gogh

People of modernity are, on average, living lives of loneliness the likes of which humanity has rarely, if ever, seen before. To be alone or to be in solitude has no valence one way or the other whereas loneliness is isolation and unmet yearning, a need as deep as hunger and thirst.
